About This Website

The Website Overview

This website is built for diabetes patients and clinicians to effectively manage and communicate with each other. With this website, clinicians can manage their patients, harvest health data and set suggested range for each patient. They can dynamically change what data need to be recorded for each individual patient and provide each patient most personal health monitor plan. Patients can upload data suggested by their clinicians and keep track with their all-time long data records to supervise their health status. Patients also will be able to attach comment on any data they record so that they can recall what happened at that time. Please log in to access all features.
